About the dataset¶

This dataset does not contain null values, the null vlaues at the "Driver id" column is equal to the number of Status "No Cars Available" and this is normal because there is no driver for these trips, also the sum of null values at the "Drop timestamp" column is equal to the sumber of Status " No Cars Available & Cancelled" and this is normal too because these trips does not comlete too so there are no drop point ("drop timestamp").¶

Conclusion¶

- To increase the Tirps and the service for users more drivers should be directed at the Airport pickup point in this period 5:00 pm to 10:00 pm, this would increase the number of completed trips by providing more cars at these peak times.¶

- Also more drivers should be directed at the City pickup point in this periods 4:00am to 11:00am, 2:00 pm to 4:00pm , and 10:00pm to 11:00pm.¶

Note that:¶

- Company must direct most of the drivers for the Airport pickup point more than the drivers who would go for the peak times for the city pickup point due to the huge need for the Airport Pickup point than the need for the City as shown at the above plots.¶

- Also we could not find a reason for the cancelled trips due to the trips duration for both pickup point is normal so we could guess these requests as a data that could bias these analysis, thefore excluded from our data, as not to affect our decisions.¶
